Fix a radiator gland nut

This water leak can be found on top of the radiator head. Just be easy with this DIY fixing, don't splash all the water to the ceiling. You can slowly tighten the gland nut. Also, use sealing paste or PTFE tape.This solution is not always working but you can try it. If it doesn't work, just replace the body of the valve or call a pro who will do the work for you.
